The great peoples of Hyrule are entitled to the very best in news coverage. We send out our reporters around our vast continent to uncover all possible leads when it comes to news. Featured Citizen

Pikango

The Artist

We took some time to track down this famous painter. Renown for being in the most interesting and dangerous locations, we happended upon Pikango in Kakariko Village! He happened to be painting the Goddess statue in the grotto there. Quite moving really!


Featured Citizen

Kanny

The Shroom Merchant of Tabantha

You wouldn't believe the lengths some people will go to make a few rupies! Kanny is pretty famous for working the stretches of road between Snowfield Stable and Serenne Stable. You might be asking yourself... who would wander out there? Kanny was happy to fill our reporters pad with his daring adventures selling Shrooms on this cold and desolate highway!
